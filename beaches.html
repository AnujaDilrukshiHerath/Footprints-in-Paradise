Destinations in the Sandy Beaches of Sri Lanka

Unawatuna

Unawatuna is a city located 140 km south of Colombo. The beach is semi-circular and stretches approximately one kilometer. It is also known for its sea turtle population and tourists are able to witness them laying eggs along the shore at various times during the year. Although Unawatuna suffered heavily in the 2004 Indian Ocean tsunami, it was rebuilt quickly and touristic activity resumed. This is a must visit spot filled with activities like surfing,diving,excursions at CoCo Bay and whale watching.

Bentota

Bentota is a city located 64 km south of Colombo and approximately 101 km south of Bandaranaike International Airport. It is largely a rural area and the land is dominated by tall coconut palm trees. It can be reached by the Colombo-Galle main road (A2) as well as the Colombo-Galle-Matara Southern Railway line. This is a must visit spot filled with activities like surfing,diving,cycling and whale watching.

Mirissa

Mirissa is a beach located close to the southern tip of Sri Lanka, approximately 200 km from the equator,and 4 km southeast of Weligama, a town on the south coast of Sri Lanka. It is crescent-shaped and secluded.This is a must visit spot filled with activities like surfing,diving,scuba driving and whale watching.

Hikkaduwa

Hikkaduwa is a small town on the south coast of Sri Lanka about 14 km northwest of Galle. It is Sri Lanka's most developed beach resort, well known for surfing and sea turtles. The surrounding waters contain a coral sanctuary that is a large shallow body of water enclosed by a reef. There is also a small collection of small islets off the beach.This is a must visit spot filled with activities like surfing,snorkeling,river safari and whale watching.
