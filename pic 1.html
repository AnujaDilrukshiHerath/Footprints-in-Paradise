"Tourist Place in Srilanka"

Sigiriya

Sigiriya is one of the most valuable historical monument of Sri lanka.Referred by locals as the Eighth Wonder of the World this ancient palace and fortress complex has significant archaeological importance and attracts thousands of tourists every year. It is probably the most visited tourist destination of Sri Lanka.
